Every new account starts on the **Trial** for 7 days. The Trial isn't a plan you pick; it's the default starting state. From the **Billing** page in your account settings you can switch to **Basic**, **Silver**, or **Platinum**.

## What each plan includes

| | Trial | Basic | Silver | Platinum |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Price | Free | $9/month | $29/month | $59/month |
| Duration | 7 days | Monthly | Monthly | Monthly |
| Frame size | 1 vCPU / 1Gi | 1 vCPU / 1Gi | 2 vCPU / 4Gi | 4 vCPU / 8Gi |
| Build power | Standard | Standard | Standard | Faster (2 vCPU / 4Gi build vs 500m / 1Gi) |
| Volumes | 1 volume, 50 GB each, 50 GB total | 3 volumes, 100 GB each, 200 GB total | 10 volumes, 500 GB each, 2000 GB total | 50 volumes, 1024 GB each, 10240 GB total |
| Custom domains | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Build priority | Standard | Standard | Standard | Priority queue |
| Support | Community | Community | Community | Priority email |
| Build minutes / month | 300 | 1,000 | 3,000 | 6,000 |
| Max build duration | 30 min | 60 min | 90 min | 120 min |

Every new account starts on the Trial, which includes 300 build minutes per month and uses the same per-container limits as Basic (1 vCPU / 1Gi), so you can build something real before you commit. When the 7 days are up, switch to a paid plan to keep deploying.

### Team seats

Add team members to your account. Each seat includes additional build minutes, a monthly usage credit, and access to projects you grant them.

| | Basic | Silver | Platinum |
|---|---|---|---|
| Price per seat | $4/month | $7/month | $12/month |
| Build minutes per seat | +500 min | +1,000 min | +2,000 min |
| Usage credit per seat | $4/month | $7/month | $12/month |

Team members' builds on granted projects draw from the project owner's monthly build-minute allowance. If the owner has a spend cap enabled (see Overage below), it applies across all builds by all team members on all projects.

### Overage

By default, builds block when you reach your monthly build-minute allowance. If you enable overage, you can keep building at $0.01 per minute beyond your allowance. Trial accounts cannot enable overage.

When you enable overage, you can optionally set a monthly spend cap. Once you hit the cap, builds block again. You can adjust your spend cap or disable overage at any time from the Billing page.

## The Billing page

The **Billing** page (under your account settings) is where you'll find:

- Your current plan and renewal date.
- Buttons to upgrade, downgrade, or cancel.
- Invoice history and payment methods.
- A live view of your usage (vCPU-hours, memory GB-hours, egress, and storage).

Plan changes happen through Stripe. Cards, invoices, and receipts are managed there too.

## What happens if a payment fails

We don't pull the rug. If a charge doesn't go through, your account moves into a **7-day grace period**. Your Frames keep running and you'll see a banner in the dashboard.

If you sort the payment out during grace, everything goes back to normal automatically. If grace expires without a successful charge, the account moves to **suspended**: your Frames are paused and deploys are blocked until you update your payment method or pick a new plan.

You won't lose data. Volumes, environment variables, custom domains, deploy history, and your repos all stay where they are. Resume the account and the Frames come back online on the next reconcile.

## Switching plans

You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades also take effect immediately and are prorated on your next invoice. Cancellation stops renewal at the end of the cycle, and you keep full access until then. Once the cycle ends, the account is canceled: your Frames are paused, deploys are blocked, and your data is preserved for 30 days.

## FAQ

**Can I change plans mid-cycle?**
Yes. Upgrades are prorated and applied immediately. Downgrades also take effect immediately and are prorated on your next invoice.

**Does the monthly usage credit roll over?**
No. Each plan includes a monthly usage credit that resets each cycle and does not roll over.

**What counts as a build minute?**
A build minute is one minute of build-pod runtime. Queue time is free; you only pay for the time your build is actually running, and usage accrues per second. Only billed overage is rounded up to whole minutes on your invoice.

**How do team-member builds affect my allowance?**
Team members' builds on projects you've granted them access to draw from your project owner allowance. The build-minute usage is pooled at the account level, not per user. If you have a spend cap enabled, it applies across all builds by any team member.

**What happens when I reach my build-minute allowance?**
By default, builds block and you'll see an error. You can enable overage from your Billing page to keep building at $0.01 per minute. You can optionally set a monthly spend cap when you enable overage.

**Do unused build minutes roll over to the next month?**
No. Your build-minute allowance resets at the start of each monthly billing cycle. Unused minutes do not carry forward.

**What happens to my volumes if I downgrade?**
Existing volumes keep working. If you go below your new plan's caps, you won't be able to create new volumes until you're under the limit.

**How do I cancel?**
Open the **Billing** page and click **Cancel plan**. Renewal stops at the end of the cycle and you keep full access until then. After the cycle ends the account is canceled: Frames are paused, deploys are blocked, and your data is preserved for 30 days.
